Frequently Asked Questions

How long does visa processing take?

Medical visa processing typically takes 3-5 working days after submission of all required documents. We provide complete assistance with the visa application process.

Can someone travel with the patient?

Yes, we encourage patients to be accompanied by a family member or friend. We can assist with visa applications for companions and arrange accommodation for them.

What is typical recovery time?

Recovery time varies depending on the procedure. Minor surgeries may require 1-2 weeks, while major procedures might need 4-6 weeks. Our doctors will provide a detailed recovery plan.

How do I pay for treatment?

We accept multiple payment methods including bank transfers, credit cards, and online payments. Payment is typically made in stages - an initial deposit and the balance before discharge.
